About Me

Welcome to my website! It's taken a lot of hard work and time, but it's finally here. Authenticity is very important to me, so of course I applied my philosophy to this project. I wanted my website to be authentic, so I tried to build almost every aspect by myself. Not from a template. Templates are easy to come by.

A little bit about me:

Come May 2016, I will be a graduate from The University of Texas at Austin, with a Bachelor of Arts in Philosophy accompanied by a Business Certificate from the Red McCombs School of Business.

I discovered my passion for video, media, and editing early on in my high school career through the Media Tech Program offered. Over the timespan of three years, I became proficient in programs such as Final Cut Pro, Photoshop, and Flash. Being involved in various projects, some responsibilities include: writing and directing music videos; hosting, filming, and editing video segments to be provided for the daily broadcast.

Further pursuing my passion for media led me to The University of Texas at Austin. While in college, I have worked on various freelance projects ranging from wedding videos to short films.

Among the Grand Prairie Independent School District I have filmed and edited various music performances such as the Grand Prairie High School Mariachi Concerts and the Garner Fine Arts Academy musicals and performances.

As well as video, I’ve done photography work for German artist Michael Just. Shown at his art exhibit “What’s Done Cannot be Undone” of January 2015 at the Cydonia Gallery in Dallas, TX, photos were taken of the Lady Macbeth sculpture found at the Elisabet Ney Museum for Just’s work.
