About Me

Welcome to my website! It's taken a lot of hard work and time, but it's finally here. Authenticity is very important to me, so of course I applied my philosophy to this project. I wanted my website to be authentic, so I tried to build almost every aspect by myself. Not from a template. Templates are easy to come by.

A little bit about me:

I am a soon-to-be graduate from The University of Texas at Austin, with a Bachelor of Arts in Philosophy accompanied by a Business Certificate from the Red McCombs School of Business.

I discovered my passion for media, editing, and film in high school through a Media Tech Program. Being involved in the program for three consecutive years, I learned the basics in programs such as: Final Cut Pro, Flash, and Photoshop. Projects ranged from writing and directing music videos, to hosting, filming and editing video segments for the school-wide news show.

I primarily moved to Austin to further pursue my passion for media. While in college, I’ve done various freelance projects varying from weddings to short films.

When it comes to new things, I adapt very quickly! For example with this website, I learned the very basics of coding an HTML site in an MIS course about a year ago. Setting my creative gears in motion, I wanted to learn more, and I found several resources like code academy to teach myself more advanced coding techniques.

I like understanding how things work, so I don’t mind taking the initiative to further my knowledge of the subject, whether it be a program or a project.

In my projects, I love having the opportunity to tell a story and express creativity. Through a platform like video I am given more than enough room to be able to do so.

Being able to pour myself into my career is something I’m looking for.
